Pizza Recipe

Below is the recipe for a pepperoni and mushroom pizza.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Basil
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Roll out dough into circular shape
  2. Apply tomato sauce evenly to dough
  3. Spread Pepperoni and Mushrooms around dough
  4. Sprinkle Mozzarella cheese on top
  5. Cook pizza in 425F pre-heated oven for 12-15 mins or until cheese is bubbling
  6. Sprinkle basil on top of pizza
